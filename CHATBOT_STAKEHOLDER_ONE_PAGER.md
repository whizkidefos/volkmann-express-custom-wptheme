# Volkmann Express Chatbot: Stakeholder One-Pager

## Purpose
The Volkmann Express chatbot helps website visitors get fast, accurate answers about services, industries, compliance support, engagement process, and contact pathways.

## Why This Matters
- Improves visitor experience with immediate responses.
- Reduces repetitive inbound questions for the team.
- Increases conversion potential by guiding users to contact actions.
- Keeps messaging consistent across common topics.

## How It Is Managed
The chatbot can be updated by non-developers in WordPress admin.

Update path:
- WordPress Admin -> VE Leads -> Chatbot Q&A

No code deployment is required for routine training updates.

## How Training Works (Non-Technical)
- Content owners add likely customer questions as keyword phrases.
- They provide a clear approved answer.
- The system matches user questions to the best-fit trained answer.
- If no strong match is found, built-in fallback responses are used.

## Editorial Standards
- Use customer language, not internal jargon.
- Keep answers short, clear, and action-oriented.
- Add a contact call-to-action when human follow-up is needed.
- Avoid uncertain or unverified claims.

## Governance Rhythm
Recommended weekly cadence:
- Review chat logs for unanswered or weakly answered queries.
- Add or refine top missing intents.
- Validate pricing/compliance wording is current.
- Retire outdated responses.

## Success Indicators
- Higher rate of accurate first responses.
- Reduced repeated support-style website inquiries.
- Increased click-through to Contact/Sales pathways.
- Fewer escalations due to unclear messaging.

## Risks And Mitigations
- Risk: Outdated answers.
- Mitigation: Weekly content review and approval ownership.

- Risk: Generic answers on niche questions.
- Mitigation: Add targeted training rows from real chat logs.

- Risk: Inconsistent tone.
- Mitigation: Maintain one editorial owner and style checklist.

## Ownership
Suggested ownership model:
- Product Owner: content direction and approval.
- Marketing/Content: ongoing training updates.
- Technical Team: periodic reliability improvements and monitoring.

## Current State
- Chatbot training is live and editable in admin.
- Matching has been improved for phrase variation and minor typos.
- Custom Q&A entries are prioritized in tie situations.
